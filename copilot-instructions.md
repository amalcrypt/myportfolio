## Next Steps - Customize Your Portfolio

### 1. Update Your Information
- Edit page files in `src/pages/` with your details
- Replace placeholder text and images
- Update your initials in the Home page hero section

### 2. Add Your Projects
- Update `src/pages/Projects.jsx` with your actual projects
- Add project descriptions, technologies, and links
- Replace placeholder images with screenshots

### 3. Configure Contact
- Update email, phone, and location in `src/pages/Contact.jsx`
- Replace social media links in Navigation and Footer
- Optional: Connect to an email service (EmailJS, Formspree, etc.)

### 4. Customize Colors
- Edit `tailwind.config.js` to change primary/secondary colors
- Modify the color scheme to match your brand

### 5. Add More Content
- Customize sections in each page component
- Add new sections if needed
- Adjust styling using Tailwind CSS classes
